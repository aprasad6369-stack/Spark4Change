Navigating Licenses & Permits

Step-by-step

1. Check what licenses & permits you need

Requirements depend on your location and the type of business you run (e.g., food service vs. consulting).

  • Use the SBA guide to understand federal, state, and local permits:

  • For California-specific registrations and tax-related permits:

San Diego Focus

2. Align with Local Rules in San Diego

In San Diego, zoning and local licensing affect where and how you can operate.

  • SBA San Diego District guidance on licensing and zoning:
    Doing business in the San Diego District (SBA) :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}
  • Connect with local business license and zoning offices when choosing a site for your business.
